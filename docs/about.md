# About

## MyoToolkit

MyoToolkit is a computational toolkit for skeletal muscle research, developed by the [HuashanMuscle](https://huashanmuscle.com/) team and the [MyoFin](https://research.folkhalsan.fi/genetics/myofin) team. It integrates three complementary tools for comprehensive muscle assessment:

- **MyoScore** — Transcriptome-based muscle health scoring
- **MyoPath** — Automated muscle pathology analysis
- **MyoSpectrum** — Integrative transcriptional atlas of human skeletal muscles

## Teams

- [HuashanMuscle](https://huashanmuscle.com/) — Huashan Hospital, Fudan University
- [MyoFin](https://research.folkhalsan.fi/genetics/myofin) — Folkhälsan Research Center, University of Helsinki
