# About

## MyoToolkit

MyoToolkit is a computational toolkit for skeletal muscle research, developed by the [HuashanMuscle](https://huashanmuscle.com/) team. It integrates three complementary tools for comprehensive muscle assessment:

- **MyoScore** — Transcriptome-based muscle health scoring
- **MyoPath** — Automated muscle pathology analysis
- **ThighMRIseg** — Deep learning MRI segmentation

## Team

MyoToolkit is developed and maintained by the HuashanMuscle research group at Huashan Hospital, Fudan University.
