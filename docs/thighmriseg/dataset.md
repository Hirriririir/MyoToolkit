# Dataset

## Multiethnic Training Data

ThighMRIseg is trained on MRI data from three geographic regions covering diverse populations and myopathy conditions.

| Cohort | Origin | Samples | Conditions |
|--------|--------|---------|------------|
| **HuashanMyo** | Han Chinese | 262 | Myopathy patients and healthy controls |
| **Folkhälsan** | Finnish | 54 | LGMDR1/2, TMD, DM1, DM2, HMERF, healthy controls |
| **MyoSegmenTUM** | German | 38 | BMD, IBM, ALS, diverse myopathies |

**Total: 354 MRI scans**

## Conditions Represented

- **LGMDR1/2** — Limb-Girdle Muscular Dystrophy
- **BMD** — Becker Muscular Dystrophy
- **DM1/DM2** — Myotonic Dystrophy Type 1/2
- **TMD** — Tibial Muscular Dystrophy
- **IBM** — Inclusion Body Myositis
- **ALS** — Amyotrophic Lateral Sclerosis
- **HMERF** — Hereditary Myopathy with Early Respiratory Failure
- Healthy controls

## Annotation Protocol

- Manual segmentation by trained radiologists
- 11 individual thigh muscles annotated per scan
- Quality reviewed by senior neuromuscular specialists
